Roshan’s First Project - Galaxy Family Resort Attappadi

Roshan's Galaxy Family Resort in Attappadi offers a picturesque retreat surrounded by natural beauty. Situated just 14 km from the Siruvani River and 7.5 km from the Bhavani River, our resort provides a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. With Ooty only 70 km away and Coimbatore just 40 km away, guests have easy access to popular tourist destinations. Explore nearby attractions such as the Shooting Place Area, Waterfalls, Road Safari, and opportunities for animal watching. Don't miss the breathtaking views from the Malleshwaram View Temple and the spiritual serenity of the Isha Yoga Foundation in Coimbatore. Conveniently located 45 km from the Coimbatore Railway Station, Galaxy Family Resort Attappadi offers a tranquil oasis for relaxation and exploration.

Roshan's Second Project: Galaxy Resort in Neelagiri

Welcome to Roshan Villa Resort, our maiden villa project nestled in the breathtaking landscapes of Neelagiri District. As a part of our commitment to excellence, we're delighted to introduce our second project, also the Galaxy Family Resort in Neelagiri. Ooty, a stunning hill station in Tamil Nadu, serves as the backdrop for these luxurious resorts. Surrounded by the majestic Nilgiris, lush fir trees, and tranquil lakes, Ooty is truly a paradise for nature enthusiasts. English, Hindi, and Tamil are widely spoken, making it accessible to visitors from all over the country. The best time to visit Ooty is during the summer season, from March to June, when the weather is pleasantly cool.
