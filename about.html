About Us

Roshan Villa Resort Developers presents our maiden villa project in Neelagiri District - Roshan Villa Resort. Roshan Villa Resort provides two different styles of Three-star living located at Neelagiri, just 15 km from Bhavani Lake. We offer 20 cottages in 5 acres of land with Mannarkkad to Neelagiri Dist. Ooty roads in a misty location accessible to all parts of Neelagiri Dist Ooty. Starting from just Rs. 35 lakh, we provide an opportunity for you to start an organic farming culture on your own land. Your investment can give you an assured monthly return in the form of rent or from the sale of farming products. We provide resort management facilities and farming assistance to ensure your monthly returns. Our facilities and cottages are designed with Three-star facilities to maximize rental income. Our club house with pool area facilities is designed for both business and family meetings. Nearly 30 cents of land is earmarked for the club house and pool with a children's play area. Our club house has indoor game zones, a party area, poolside barbecue counter, etc.
